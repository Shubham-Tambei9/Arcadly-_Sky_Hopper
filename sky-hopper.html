About the Game

Sky Hopper is the ultimate test of your reflexes and timing. In this high-flying arcade adventure, you control a vibrant bird navigating through a treacherous sky filled with obstacles.

Master the "One-Tap" mechanics to soar between pipes and climb the global leaderboards. Every run is procedurally generated, ensuring no two flights are ever the same. Crafted with a vibrant neo-retro aesthetic that pops off the screen.
